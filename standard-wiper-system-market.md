<p><p>The standard wiper system market consists of three main types: wiper motors, windshield wipers, and rain sensors. Wiper motors are electric devices that drive the wiper blades, providing the necessary force for optimal performance. Windshield wipers are the blades that physically remove water and debris from the windshield surface. Rain sensors enhance functionality by automatically activating the wipers when moisture is detected, improving driver visibility and safety. Together, these components form an essential system for vehicle maintenance and driver comfort.</p></p>
