<p><p>The commercial vehicle EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) system market includes two main types of valves: electric EGR valves and pneumatic EGR valves. </p><p>Electric EGR valves utilize electrical signals to control the flow of exhaust gases, providing precise regulation and faster response times. In contrast, pneumatic EGR valves rely on air pressure for operation, making them simpler but potentially less efficient. Both types play crucial roles in reducing emissions and improving engine performance in commercial vehicles, catering to different operational needs and technological preferences.</p></p>
