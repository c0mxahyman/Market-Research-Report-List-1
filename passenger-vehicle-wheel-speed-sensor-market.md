<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Passenger Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor market features two primary types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel rotation, providing precise speed data and enabling advanced traction control systems. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors generate voltage signals in response to wheel motion, offering a simpler and cost-effective solution for speed measurement. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety and performance by enabling features like anti-lock braking systems and electronic stability control.</p></p>
