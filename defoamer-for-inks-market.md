<p><p>The defoamer for inks market is segmented into Type I and Type II. Type I defoamers are primarily oil-based, offering high efficiency in reducing surface tension and preventing foam in solvent-based inks. They are commonly used in traditional printing processes. Type II defoamers, on the other hand, are water-based and environmentally friendly, optimized for use in aqueous and UV-cured inks. This segmentation allows manufacturers to select defoamers that best suit their ink formulations and production requirements, enhancing print quality and efficiency.</p></p>
