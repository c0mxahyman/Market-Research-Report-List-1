<p><p>Skin-effect heat-tracing cables are specialized electrical cables designed to provide heat to maintain the temperature of materials in pipelines and vessels. These cables utilize the skin effect principle, wherein electric current flows more on the surface of the conductor than through its core, effectively generating heat along its length. This technology is particularly advantageous in industrial settings for preventing the freezing of fluids or maintaining optimal viscosities in chemical processes.</p><p>The Skin-Effect Heat-Tracing Cables Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 5.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand across various sectors, including oil and gas, chemical processing, and food and beverage.
